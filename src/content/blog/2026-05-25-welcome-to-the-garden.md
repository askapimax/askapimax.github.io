This is the first turn of the soil: a personal place for thoughts, notes, and projects that do not need to be perfectly sorted right away.

To me, a digital garden is less of an archive and more of a working surface. Some texts will become essays, some will stay sketches, and some will grow in a new direction over several weeks.

## Why Markdown?

Markdown keeps things pleasantly simple. The texts are readable, versioned, and stored directly in the repository. That is a good foundation because it is not tied to a specific CMS.

## What can grow here

- Thoughts on technology and productivity
- Project notes from everyday work
- Small essays about things that stay with me
- Experiments with the web, design, and automation

The garden is open. It only needs to be watered regularly.
