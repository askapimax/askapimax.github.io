Many good thoughts disappear because they are judged too early. A blog can be an antidote when it is not treated only as a showcase.

Here, texts are allowed to begin unfinished. A paragraph, an observation, a question. Later, it can become more.

> Not every note has to become an article. But every good note deserves a place where it can be found again.

The goal is not to publish something big every day. The goal is to leave a trail.
